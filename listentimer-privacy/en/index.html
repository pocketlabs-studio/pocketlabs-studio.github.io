Privacy Policy

Effective: March 22, 2026 | Last Updated: March 22, 2026

PocketLabs ("we," "us," or "our") values the privacy of users of ListenTimer (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal information the App collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

Category Data Collected Storage
App Settings Timer presets, voice character, voice interval, haptic settings Stored locally on your device (never sent to any server)
Advertising Data (free users) Advertising identifier (IDFA), device information, app usage data Google LLC servers
This App does not operate its own servers. We do not collect account information, login credentials, or require registration.

2. How We Collect Information

  • App Settings: Values you configure within the App are automatically saved on your device.
  • Advertising Data: Collected automatically by the Google AdMob SDK, based on your App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent. If you do not allow tracking via ATT, your IDFA is not collected.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

  • App Settings: To provide a personalized timer experience
  • Advertising Data: To deliver personalized ads and measure advertising performance

4. Data Retention

Category Retention Period
App Settings (local) Automatically deleted when you uninstall the App
Advertising Data Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

5. Third-Party Data Sharing

Recipient Data Shared Purpose
Google LLC (AdMob) Advertising identifier (IDFA), device information, app usage data Personalized advertising and performance measurement

When you purchase Premium, ads are removed and data sharing with Google ceases.

6. International Data Transfer

Advertising data is transferred to Google LLC servers located in the United States and other countries. Google applies appropriate safeguards under the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework. For details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Withdraw consent for data collection
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information
  • Request suspension of data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@pocketlabs.kr. We will respond without delay.

8. Data Deletion

  • App Settings (local data): All settings stored on your device are automatically deleted when you uninstall the App.
  • Advertising Data: Handled according to Google's data retention policies. You can stop future data collection by changing your ATT settings.

9. Data Protection Officer

Item Details
Contact Privacy Team
Email contact@pocketlabs.kr

10. Security Measures

We take the following measures to ensure the security of your personal information:

  • We do not operate our own servers, eliminating risks of server breaches or data leaks.
  • All app settings are stored locally on your device only.
  • Communication with ad networks is encrypted via HTTPS.

11. Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 14 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). If we become aware that personal information from a child has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes with Premium Purchase

When you purchase Premium, in-app ads are removed. As a result, advertising data collection through the AdMob SDK (IDFA, device information, app usage data) ceases.

13. Withdrawing Consent

You can withdraw your consent for ad tracking at any time through the following steps:

iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > Toggle off ListenTimer

Disabling tracking will stop IDFA-based personalized advertising data collection.

14. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, we will post the changes on this page and update the effective date. For significant changes, we may provide notice within the App.
